Women & Youth Empowerment

Building the next generation of female leaders and equipping youth with skills for tomorrow's challenges

Our Empowerment Programs

SIRAD's initiatives are designed to break barriers and create opportunities for women and youth through education, mentorship, and practical skill development.

Women in STEM Leadership

  • Mentorship programs with industry leaders
  • Technical skills development workshops
  • Career pathway guidance
  • Networking opportunities

Youth Innovation Labs

  • Hands-on technology training
  • Entrepreneurship development
  • Design thinking workshops
  • Project incubation support

Community Leadership

  • Grassroots organizing skills
  • Advocacy training
  • Public speaking development
  • Social impact project design

Our Impact

Since 2018, our programs have trained over 2,500 women and youth participants, with 78% of graduates reporting career advancement or successful entrepreneurial ventures within one year of completing our programs.

Success Stories
