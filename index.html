Explore Our Work
The SIRAD Institute for Research and Development (SIRAD) is a leading civil society organization in Ethiopia dedicated to uniting communities to develop and implement sustainable, evidence-based solutions for peace, social cohesion, Capacity building and empowering women and youth for inclusive development. In addition to its peacebuilding efforts, SIRAD also operates as a research consultant firm and policy advisory institute.

Our Mission

We unite communities in developing sustainable solutions that promote peace, human rights, and social cohesion while serving as a leading research and policy advisory institute.

Building Peace from the Ground Up

SIRAD bridges the gap between grassroots knowledge and policy-making. We don't just study conflict - we equip Ethiopians with the tools to solve it through:

  • Community-led peacebuilding initiatives
  • Cutting-edge conflict research
  • Policy frameworks adopted by governments
  • Capacity building for sustainable impact
